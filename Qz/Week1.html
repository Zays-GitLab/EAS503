5. What are augmented assignments? Give an example.

Augmented assignment operators in Python programming are basically combinations of the functionality of an arithmetic or bitwise operator with the assignment operator. For example “x+=1” is “x=x+1” and “x*=1” is “x=x*1”.

1. What is a simultaneous assignment? Give an example. Give an example of when they are used.

Simultaneous assignment is when you can make multiple assignments on the same line in scripting. For example “x, y, z = 2 ,[2,3],{“UB”:3}” makes “x = 2” with “y = [2,3]” and z = {“UB”:3}.
